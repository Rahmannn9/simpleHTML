About Us

Summer Tour is a large full-service travel company operating in Turkey, founded in 2002 and mainly providing services to clients from Germany, The Netherlands, Belgium, Romania, Bulgaria, Portugal, Moldova, Russia, Azerbaijan and the Scandinavian countries. Offices of Summer Tour are located in all strategically important resort cities of Turkey: headquartered in Antalya as well as having offices in Alanya, Side, Bodrum, Marmaris and Kusadasi.

The main sphere of the company's activity is the reception of tourists. Summer Tour’s own B2B online resources allowed the company to significantly simplify sales in this segment. Over the past 10 years, the company has served more than 9 million tourists. In addition to being one of the top 10 DMCs in Turkey, Summer Tour also owns hotels on the coast of Antalya, Istanbul and Germany.
